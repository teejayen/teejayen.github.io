Coding.

Exercising.

Flying.

Journaling.

Learning.

Listening.

Meditating.

Practicing.

Reading.

Researching.

Selling.

Testing.

Working.

Writing.

I want to do it all, and I want to do it now.. I don't _need_ to do it all, and even if I want to, I need to acknowledge that I **can't** do it all. 

Sometimes I need to step back and realise that **Being.** is enough.

Our time and energy is finite, make it count.
